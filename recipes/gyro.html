Gyro

Description

Gyros—in some regions, chiefly North America, anglicized as a gyro —is meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie, then sliced and served wrapped or stuffed in pita bread, along with ingredients such as tomato, onion, fried potatoes, and tzatziki.

Ingredients

  • Tzatziki
  • Bread
  • Lamb & Mutton
  • Onion
  • Garlic

How to Cook

  1. Place a pita bread on foil
  2. Spread with Tzatziki
  3. Add meat, cheese, and vegetables
  4. Roll sandwich up and wrap in foil
  5. Enjoy it!
